# Writing Articles

This folder contains materials, tools, and guidance for writing academic articles.
This is a collection that I will slowly update over time. It is intended to support:
- Researchers who want to improve their academic writing
- Students learning how to write scientific papers
- Anyone interested in clearer and more structured writing

My goal is to provide simple, practical, and reusable tools that make academic writing easier and more effective.

## What you will find
- Templates (e.g. rebuttals, papers)
- Writing tips and examples
- Structured approaches for academic writing
- Tools and resources


Feel free to explore and adapt the materials for your own work.
