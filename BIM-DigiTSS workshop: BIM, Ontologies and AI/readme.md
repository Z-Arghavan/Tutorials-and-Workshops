# Advancing Circular Construction: Integrating BIM, Ontologies, and AI

## Short Description

Circular construction requires structured knowledge that can be exchanged across digital systems such as BIM, material banks, and digital twins.  

The **first session** introduces the concepts of circularity and reusability in the built environment and explains how information models and data models support information exchange between systems, for example between BIM models and material banks. This session uses the **Decommissioning and Reuse Ontology (DOR)** and **bSDD** as examples.

The **second session** is a hands-on exploration where participants learn the basics of semantic web technologies and ontology design. Students will interact with an existing ontology, explore its structure, identify modelling gaps, and experiment with extending it. The session also briefly introduces how AI can support ontology querying and development for circular construction.
